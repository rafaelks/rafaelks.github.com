I've started two digital companies in the past, both failed, and now I'm trying my third one with my friend Thiago Sanchez. I always had to move files around, search for tickets on my email, look for the hotel address in another application and there was still some application that didn't support offline state very well... so we started Tripsy.

If you like to travel around, please make sure to download Tripsy on the App Store and give us the feedback of your usage, that would be important for us to create a great product.
